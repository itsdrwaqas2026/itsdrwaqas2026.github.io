Welcome to Dr. Muhammad Waqas Homepage

I am currently serving as Director (Agronomy & Extension), BPS-19 at the Department of Agriculture Extension, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, and Manager of the Trichogramma Rearing Laboratory — a key biological control facility in the region. With 17+ years of combined research and government service, my work bridges cutting-edge plant science with practical agricultural extension.

My research focuses on endophytic fungi, plant-microbe interactions, phytohormones, bioremediation of heavy metals, and integrated pest management. I completed my PhD in Agronomy (Crop Physiology) at Kyungpook National University, South Korea, followed by a Post-Doctoral Fellowship (Brain Korea BK-21 Plus) and an Invited Research Professorship at the same institution (2015–2017).

My research has generated over 10,000 citations and an h-index of 54, with publications in Plant Physiology and Biochemistry, Biology and Fertility of Soils, Science of the Total Environment, PLoS ONE, and more.

Research interests: Agronomy · Plant Physiology · Endophytic Fungi · Plant-Microbe Interaction · Abiotic & Biotic Stress · Phytohormones & Secondary Metabolites · Heavy Metal Bioremediation · IPM · Biological Control
